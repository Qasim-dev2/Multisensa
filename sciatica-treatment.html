Sciatica Treatment

Stop Sciatica Pain - Expert Nerve Pain Relief

Sciatica can cause severe pain that radiates from your lower back down through your leg, making everyday activities nearly impossible. At Multisensa Physiotherapy, we specialize in treating sciatic nerve pain with targeted, evidence-based treatments.

Our expert physiotherapists use advanced techniques to address the root cause of your sciatica, providing lasting relief and preventing future flare-ups.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does sciatica treatment take?

Most patients experience significant relief within 4-8 weeks of consistent treatment. The duration depends on the severity and underlying cause of your sciatica.

Can sciatica be cured permanently?

Yes, with proper treatment addressing the root cause and following a prevention program, many patients achieve long-term relief from sciatica. We teach you exercises and lifestyle modifications for lasting results.

What makes sciatica worse?

Prolonged sitting, heavy lifting with poor form, and lack of physical activity can worsen sciatica. We'll identify your specific triggers and help you modify activities to reduce pain.
