Knee Pain Therapy

Get Moving Again - Expert Knee Pain Treatment

Knee pain can severely limit your mobility and quality of life. Whether from injury, arthritis, or overuse, our specialized physiotherapy treatments help restore knee function and reduce pain effectively.

At Multisensa, we provide comprehensive knee rehabilitation programs tailored to your specific condition and goals.

Common Knee Conditions We Treat

Osteoarthritis
ACL/PCL Injuries
Meniscus Tears
Patellofemoral Pain
Runner's Knee
Post-Surgery Rehabilitation
Ligament Sprains
Knee Bursitis

Our Knee Recovery Strategy

1

Knee Assessment

Complete evaluation of knee stability, range of motion, and muscle strength.

2

Pain Management

Manual therapy and modalities to reduce inflammation and pain levels.

3

Strengthening Program

Progressive exercises to rebuild quadriceps, hamstrings, and knee stability.

4

Functional Training

Return to daily activities and sports with proper movement patterns.
