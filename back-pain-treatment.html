Frequently Asked Questions

How long does back pain treatment take?

Treatment duration varies depending on the severity and cause of your back pain. Acute back pain may improve in 2-4 weeks, while chronic conditions may require 6-12 weeks of treatment. We'll provide a realistic timeline during your initial assessment.

Is physiotherapy painful for back pain?

Our treatments are designed to reduce pain, not increase it. While you may experience some mild discomfort during certain exercises, we work within your comfort level and gradually progress your treatment.

Do I need surgery for my back pain?

Most back pain conditions can be effectively treated with physiotherapy without surgery. Studies show that conservative treatment is often as effective as surgery for many back problems, with fewer risks and complications.

Can I prevent back pain from returning?

Yes! We provide education on proper posture, ergonomics, and exercises to strengthen your back and core muscles. Following our prevention strategies significantly reduces the risk of recurring back pain.
