Frequently Asked Questions

How long does neck pain treatment take?

Acute neck pain often improves within 1-3 weeks with proper treatment. Chronic conditions or those with underlying issues may require 6-8 weeks. We'll assess your specific condition and provide a realistic timeline.

What causes tech neck and how can it be treated?

Tech neck results from prolonged forward head posture while using devices. We treat it through postural correction exercises, strengthening deep neck muscles, and ergonomic education to prevent reoccurrence.

Is physiotherapy safe for whiplash injuries?

Yes, physiotherapy is highly effective for whiplash. We use gentle techniques initially, then progressively restore movement and strength as healing progresses. Early physiotherapy intervention often leads to better outcomes.

Can neck pain cause headaches?

Yes, cervicogenic headaches originate from neck problems. Our treatment addresses both neck dysfunction and associated headaches through targeted manual therapy and exercises, often providing significant relief.
