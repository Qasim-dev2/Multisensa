Our Process

How We Treat You

Our systematic 6-step approach ensures the best possible outcome for your complete recovery

Step 01

Patient Assessment

We thoroughly evaluate your condition, medical history, symptoms, and lifestyle to understand your unique situation.

Step 02

Pain Diagnosis

Pinpointing the exact source and cause of your discomfort using advanced diagnostic techniques and clinical testing.

Step 03

Personalized Therapy Plan

Creating a custom recovery roadmap tailored specifically to your health goals and treatment needs.

Your Recovery Journey
Step 04

Targeted Treatment

Manual therapy, modern equipment, and corrective exercises applied directly to address your condition.

Step 05

Recovery & Rehabilitation

Restoring your range of motion, building strength, and regaining confidence through guided exercises.

Step 06

Long-term Prevention

Posture correction, lifestyle guidance, and exercise routines to prevent pain recurrence.
